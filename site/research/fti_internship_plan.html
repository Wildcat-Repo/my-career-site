FTI Consulting Internship Preparation Plan

Strategic 12-Week Roadmap for Technology Segment Success

📋 Executive Summary

This comprehensive 12-week preparation plan positions me strategically for FTI Consulting's Technology segment internship program. The plan integrates deep company research, targeted skill development, portfolio enhancement, and interview preparation while building valuable professional relationships.

Key Objectives: Demonstrate technical excellence in cybersecurity and data analytics, showcase business acumen through project portfolio, and establish meaningful connections within FTI's Technology division.

🔍Phase 1: Research & Understanding (Weeks 1-2)

📚Week 1: Deep Dive into FTI Consulting

Day 1-2: Company Research

  • Read FTI's latest annual report and quarterly earnings calls
  • Study their 5 business segments in detail, focusing on Technology
  • Research FTI's major case studies and recent wins
  • Follow FTI Consulting on LinkedIn and set up Google Alerts

Day 3-4: Technology Segment Focus

  • Research FTI's Technology leadership team on LinkedIn
  • Study their cybersecurity, e-discovery, and data analytics services
  • Read recent thought leadership articles from FTI Technology experts
  • Identify specific tools and technologies FTI uses (Relativity, Nuix, etc.)

Day 5-7: Industry Understanding

  • Research the consulting industry landscape (competitors like Deloitte, EY)
  • Understand current trends in legal technology and digital forensics
  • Read about recent high-profile cases requiring technology consulting
  • Study regulatory environment (GDPR, SOX, etc.) affecting FTI's work

🎯Week 2: Application Strategy

Day 1-3: Application Materials Research

  • Find FTI's internship postings and requirements
  • Research application deadlines and process timeline
  • Identify FTI recruiters and hiring managers on LinkedIn
  • Study successful intern profiles and testimonials

Day 4-7: Networking Foundation

  • Create list of FTI employees to connect with
  • Join relevant professional groups (cybersecurity, digital forensics)
  • Research FTI's campus recruiting events and information sessions
  • Set up informational interview requests

⚡Phase 2: Skill Development & Portfolio Enhancement (Weeks 3-6)

📊Week 3: Technical Skills Assessment

Day 1-2: Skills Gap Analysis

  • Compare current skills against FTI Technology job requirements
  • Identify priority areas for improvement
  • Create learning plan for key technologies

Day 3-7: Core Skills Development

  • Digital Forensics: Complete online course (SANS, Cybrary)
  • E-Discovery: Learn about Relativity platform (free training)
  • Data Analytics: Advanced Python for forensics applications
  • Cloud Security: AWS/Azure security fundamentals

🚀Week 4: Security Project Deep Dive

  • Enhance phishing detection project with additional features
  • Add forensic analysis capabilities to demonstrate investigation skills
  • Create detailed documentation showing business impact
  • Prepare technical presentation materials

💼Week 5: Portfolio Projects

  • Document all projects with business impact metrics
  • Create GitHub portfolio with professional README files
  • Build simple portfolio website showcasing FTI-relevant work
  • Prepare project demonstration videos

🏆Week 6: Industry Certifications

  • Study for CompTIA Security+ (aligns with CYB 300 course)
  • Complete Google Cybersecurity Certificate modules
  • Pursue vendor-specific training (Microsoft Security, AWS Security)
  • Research Professional Scrum Master certification completion

📝Phase 3: Application Materials Creation (Weeks 7-8)

📄Week 7: Resume & Cover Letter

Day 1-3: Resume Development

  • Create FTI-specific resume highlighting relevant projects
  • Quantify achievements with business metrics
  • Emphasize technology skills relevant to consulting
  • Get resume reviewed by career services and professionals

Day 4-7: Cover Letter & Application Essays

  • Write compelling cover letter connecting background to FTI's needs
  • Prepare responses to common application questions
  • Draft "Why FTI?" statement demonstrating company knowledge
  • Have materials reviewed and edited

✅Week 8: Application Submission Preparation

Day 1-3: Final Application Package

  • Complete all application forms and requirements
  • Prepare portfolio of work samples
  • Gather references and recommendation letters
  • Create backup versions of all materials

Day 4-7: Quality Assurance

  • Proofread all materials multiple times
  • Test any links or online portfolios
  • Prepare submission strategy and timeline
  • Submit applications before deadlines

🎤Phase 4: Interview Preparation (Weeks 9-12)

💻Week 9: Core Technical Knowledge

  • Review fundamental CS concepts (data structures, algorithms)
  • Practice coding problems relevant to data analysis
  • Study cybersecurity frameworks and methodologies
  • Prepare to discuss projects in technical detail

🔧Week 10: Consulting-Specific Technical Skills

  • Learn about digital forensics investigation methodologies
  • Understand e-discovery workflows and challenges
  • Study data analytics in legal contexts
  • Practice explaining technical concepts to non-technical audiences

📋Week 11: Case Study & Behavioral Prep

Day 1-3: Case Study Practice

  • Practice consulting case studies (technology-focused)
  • Develop structured approach to problem-solving
  • Practice presenting solutions clearly and concisely
  • Study FTI's case study methodology

Day 4-7: Behavioral Interviews

  • Prepare STAR method responses for common questions
  • Develop stories highlighting leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving
  • Practice questions about handling pressure and tight deadlines
  • Prepare examples of client-facing or stakeholder management experience

🎯Week 12: Final Interview Preparation

Day 1-3: Mock Interviews

  • Conduct mock technical interviews
  • Practice case study presentations
  • Get feedback from mentors or career services
  • Refine answers based on feedback

Day 4-7: Interview Logistics

  • Research interview format and expectations
  • Prepare thoughtful questions about FTI's culture and opportunities
  • Plan interview day logistics (travel, timing, materials)
  • Finalize presentation materials and portfolio

🤝Phase 5: Networking & Relationship Building (Ongoing)

📅Weekly Networking Tasks

  • Send 3-5 LinkedIn connection requests to FTI employees
  • Comment thoughtfully on FTI's LinkedIn posts
  • Share relevant industry articles with insights
  • Attend virtual industry events where FTI might be present

🌟Monthly Deep Networking

  • Request informational interviews with FTI Technology professionals
  • Attend cybersecurity or legal technology conferences
  • Participate in relevant webinars and panel discussions
  • Follow up with existing connections
